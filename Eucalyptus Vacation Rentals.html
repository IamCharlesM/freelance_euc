    Referral Program Details:

    How does it work?:
    Each time a person that you refer is billed their monthly property fee (after their free trial period) , you earn a discount that is equal to 10% of the invoice amount before any of their discounts are applied.

    For example...
    The current monthly subscription fee is $29.99. If you refer 1 friend and they pay their subscription fee, we will discount your next bill $3.00. If you refer 10 friends, we will discount your next bill by $30.00! As long as they continue paying their subscription fees, you keep collecting discounts!!
    There is no limit to the number of discounts you can collect or the number of invoices we'll discount!!!

    What happens if they've referred 10 people?
    You still earn your normal discount (10% of their bill before their discounts are applied).

    What if I earn more discounts than I can use?
    Any unused portions of your earned discount are banked for future use. They are applied automatically to your next invoice.

    We know what you're thinking...
    "That's crazy! Why?!??!" Well, the answer is simple. We believe we can provide a superior service and great experience at a price everyone can afford. In short...Everyone wins!

    So, how do I refer people?
    It's simple! When they create an account, please make sure they enter your email address into the referral email box. Here's what it looks like:

    If they forget to enter your email address or you have any questions, please contact us. We're happy to help in any way we can.

    Founding Koalas Program:

    What is it?:
    Our founding members ("Founding Koalas") are able to list up to 2 properties FREE for the first year! Additionally, provided we hit our annual membership goals, the Founding Koalas may be entitled to some special gifts.

    Cool! What happens after the first year?
    Well, it depends... Provided we're able to hit our membership goals each year, we'll extend it another year. If we don't hit our performance goals, you will be billed the regular monthly subscription fee for each property.

    Ok. So what's this year's goal?
    Our goal this year is to have 400 paying subscriptions by September 30, 2019.

    Awesome! How do I become a Founding Koala?
    The first 100 members to activate a subscription will automatically become Founding Koalas. If you have any questions, please contact us.
